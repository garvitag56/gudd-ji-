Our Story

Gudd Ji was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to provide the purest and most organic jaggery products to health-conscious consumers. What started as a small family business has now grown into a trusted brand known for its commitment to quality and authenticity.

Our journey began in a small village in India, where We learned the traditional art of jaggery making from our community people who have 50 years of experience in jaggery making we have perfected the process of extracting and processing jaggery while preserving its natural goodness.

Our Mission

At Gudd Ji, our mission is to promote healthy living by offering natural sweeteners that are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. We believe in sustainable farming practices and work closely with local farmers who share our values.

We are committed to:

  • Producing 100% organic and chemical-free jaggery
  • Supporting local farmers and communities
  • Promoting sustainable agricultural practices
  • Educating consumers about the benefits of natural sweeteners

Our Process

The journey of our jaggery begins with carefully selected sugarcane, grown without the use of harmful pesticides. Our traditional extraction process ensures that all the natural minerals and nutrients are preserved.

We follow these steps to ensure the highest quality:

  1. Selection of premium sugarcane
  2. Traditional extraction process
  3. Natural clarification without chemicals
  4. Slow cooking to preserve nutrients
  5. Careful packaging to maintain freshness
